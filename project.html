PROJECT

nvigor

Compared to other tech hubs in The States such as Silicon Valley or New York, there is a big demographic gap between professionals and students in Philadelphia. We want more students to take part and collaborate with professionals for a better tech community. In order to do that, we founded nvigor. nvigor is a student-run non-profit startup for students in Greater Philadelphia area. The vision of nvigor is to invigorate and connect students to the rapidly growing startup community. We are building a sustainable and successful students and professionals network for a better Philadelphia. To learn more, check out our website nvigor.org
